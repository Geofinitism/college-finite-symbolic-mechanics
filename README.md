# College of Finite Symbolic Mechanics

The College of Finite Symbolic Mechanics develops the mathematical framework associated with Geofinitism.

It focuses on symbolic systems grounded in finite operations and measurable structures.

---

## Scope

- Finite symbolic systems  
- Alphons, nexils, symbolic geometry  
- Measurement-based mathematics  
- Alternative mathematical formulations  

---

Geofinitism is a finite-measurement philosophy of language, mathematics, and dynamical systems.
